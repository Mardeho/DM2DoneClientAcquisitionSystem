The Method

The 5-Stage Client Pipeline

Every conversation runs through the same five stages. Once you know the system, you'll never wonder what to say next.

1Find

Find — Build Your Ideal Client List

Use LinkedIn's search filters to identify exactly who to connect with. Stop scrolling randomly — start targeting strategically with the included Client Targeting Workbook.

2Connect

Connect — Send the Right Request

First impressions set the tone. Use the authority, curiosity, or referral-style connection request templates to get accepted — and remembered.

3Converse

Converse — Open Authentic Dialogue

No pitching in the opener. Use the conversation starter templates to build genuine rapport that makes the eventual call invitation feel natural, not transactional.

4Invite

Invite — Offer the Clarity Call

When the moment is right, use the call invitation templates to move the conversation forward — without pressure, without awkwardness, without being "salesy."

5Convert

Convert — Lead the Call, Close the Client

Walk into every discovery call with a proven framework. Handle objections with confidence and follow up within 24 hours with the included scripts.
